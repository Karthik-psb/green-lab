# green-lab

> An open R&D lab building AI-powered tools for Indian agriculture.

## Vision

Indian agriculture employs nearly half the country's workforce yet contributes a disproportionately small share of GDP. Yields per hectare for most staple crops lag global benchmarks, water tables are falling, input costs are rising, and climate volatility is accelerating. AI techniques commercially proven elsewhere remain largely unavailable to the average Indian farmer.

**green-lab** exists to change that. Our goal is to design, prototype, and eventually commercialize AI + automation systems that produce **measurable improvements** in yield, input efficiency, and farmer income — specifically calibrated for Indian field conditions, farm sizes, and economic realities.

## Why India, Why Now

- **~86% of Indian farmers are smallholders** (less than 2 hectares). Solutions built for large US farms do not translate. We design for the small, fragmented, mixed-cropping Indian farm.
- **Smartphone penetration is now deep in rural India**, making computer vision and advisory tools deliverable at near-zero marginal cost.
- **Water stress is acute** across major agricultural belts (Punjab, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu). Irrigation efficiency is a national priority.
- **Government and private capital are aligning**: AgriStack, ONDC for agriculture, FPO ecosystem, and rising agri-tech VC interest.
- **AI is now cheap enough to deploy at the edge** — a Jetson Nano or a mid-range Android phone can run real-time inference on field imagery.

## Focus Areas

We are tracking five sub-domains. The first MVP wedge will be chosen from this list.

- **Precision spraying and weed detection** — computer vision to identify weeds vs crops in real time; reduce herbicide use by 50 to 80 percent per pass.
- **Smartphone-based crop disease detection** — photograph a leaf, get a diagnosis and treatment recommendation in seconds.
- **AI-driven irrigation scheduling** — soil moisture, weather forecasts, and crop-stage data combined to deliver water only where and when needed.
- **Satellite and drone yield prediction** — field-level forecasts and stress alerts for FPOs, insurance companies, and lenders.
- **Autonomous weeding robotics** — chemical-free mechanical weeding for high-value crops like vegetables and fruits.

See the research/ folder for detailed notes on each vertical.
