## References

Key Indian government programs and institutions relevant to green-lab:

- AgriStack: India's national digital agriculture ecosystem (farmer registry, crop registry, credit-linked data)
  https://agricoop.nic.in/en/agristack

- ONDC for Agriculture: Open Network for Digital Commerce extension into agri value chains
  https://ondc.org/

- PM Kisan: Direct benefit transfer to farmers (database of 110 million+ farmer households)
  https://pmkisan.gov.in/

- Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY): Crop insurance scheme
  https://pmfby.gov.in/

- ICAR (Indian Council of Agricultural Research): Network of 100+ research institutes
  https://icar.org.in/

- Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs): 731 farm science centres, one per district
  https://kvk.icar.gov.in/

- SFAC (Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium): FPO registration and support
  https://sfacindia.com/

- NABARD: Primary lender and policy institution for rural India
  https://www.nabard.org/
