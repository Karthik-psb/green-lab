# Research

Domain research notes for each of the five AI + Agriculture verticals tracked by green-lab.

## Files in this folder

- vertical-comparison.md — Side-by-side assessment of all five verticals against: problem specificity, data availability, India market fit, deployment path, and willingness to pay
- india-datasets.md — Index of publicly available Indian agricultural datasets (soil, weather, satellite, crop disease images)
- 01-precision-spraying.md — Notes on weed vs crop computer vision: technology landscape, key players, applicability in India
- 02-disease-detection.md — Notes on smartphone-based crop disease detection: models, datasets, Indian deployments
- 03-irrigation-scheduling.md — Notes on AI-driven irrigation: sensor options, weather APIs, water stress in India
- 04-yield-prediction.md — Notes on satellite and drone yield prediction: data sources, model approaches, FPO applications
- 05-autonomous-weeding.md — Notes on autonomous weeding robotics: hardware cost, India feasibility, high-value crop use cases

## Research Methodology

For each vertical we answer five questions:
1. Is the problem well-defined enough for AI? (specific inputs and outputs, fast feedback loop)
2. What data exists and what do we need to collect?
3. What does the Indian smallholder context change vs global deployments?
4. What is the path from prototype to a paying customer?
5. Who are we competing with and what is our differentiation?
